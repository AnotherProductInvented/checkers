How to Play Checkers

Standard checkers is played on an 8×8 board (only dark squares are used). Each player’s pieces move diagonally forward. Capture an opponent’s piece by jumping over it. If a capture is available, you must take it. When a piece reaches the far side, it is crowned as a king and only then is allowed to move backwards, too.
